What is Sadaqah Jariyah?

Sadaqah Jariyah is a form of continuous charity that provides ongoing benefits to society even after the donor's death.

How It Works

  • Individuals contribute to projects that provide lasting impact.
  • Examples include building wells, schools, and hospitals.
